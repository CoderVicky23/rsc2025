Invitation

The Research Scholar's Conclave 2025 is set to take place at the National Institute of Technology Raipur on December 19-20, 2025, bringing together researchers, academicians, and experts from diverse fields. This year, the conclave is centered around the theme "Sustainable Technology," focusing on innovative research in Engineering, Basic Sciences, and Humanities. It aims to foster discussions on cutting-edge advancements that promote sustainability across multiple domains.

For the first time, the conclave is open to research scholars from reputed institutes across the country, encouraging healthy academic competition and collaboration. This expansion provides an excellent opportunity for scholars to present their research, engage in insightful discussions, and build meaningful professional networks. The event will feature technical sessions, panel discussions, and keynote addresses by distinguished experts, offering valuable exposure to emerging trends and developments.

A key highlight of the conclave is the Scopus-indexed proceedings, ensuring that the presented research gains global visibility and recognition. Participants will have the opportunity to contribute to high-impact discussions, receive constructive feedback, and explore potential collaborations that can drive their research forward.

With a focus on sustainability-driven innovations and interdisciplinary knowledge exchange, the Research Scholar’s Conclave 2025 promises to be an enriching experience for all attendees. We look forward to an engaging and successful event, bringing together some of the brightest minds working toward a sustainable future.

About the Event

The Office of Dean (R&C), NIT Raipur, is organizing a three-day Research Scholars' Conclave from 19-20th Dec 2025 at the NIT Raipur campus in offline mode. Under the leadership of the Dean (R&C), this year's conclave is set to witness a qualitative enhancement with the introduction of Scopus-indexed proceedings, ensuring global visibility and recognition for the presented research.

For the first time, the conclave is open to research scholars from other reputed institutes, fostering healthy competition and collaborative research opportunities. The event serves as a multidisciplinary platform for scholars to showcase their research, exchange innovative ideas, and contribute to cutting-edge advancements in sustainable and transformative technologies.

Parallel sessions will be conducted across different research domains, with expert evaluations, and the best presentations will be recognized and awarded. The conclave will act as a catalyst for impactful research and academic excellence, driving the institution towards a future rooted in sustainability and innovation.
