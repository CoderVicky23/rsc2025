Invitation

We are delighted to invite you to the Research Scholar's Conclave 2025, to be held at the National Institute of Technology Raipur on December 12-13, 2025.

The Research Scholar's Conclave provides a platform for research scholars to present their work, exchange ideas, and collaborate with peers and experts. It aims to foster a vibrant research ecosystem and promote interdisciplinary interactions.

We look forward to your participation in making this conclave a great success.

About the Event

The Research Scholar's Conclave 2025 is a premier event organized by NIT Raipur to bring together research scholars, academicians, and industry professionals from across the country.

The conclave will feature keynote speeches, paper presentations, poster sessions, panel discussions, and networking opportunities. It aims to provide a platform for showcasing cutting-edge research and fostering collaboration across disciplines.

Key areas of focus include:

  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
  • Renewable Energy and Sustainability
  • Advanced Materials and Manufacturing
  • Communications and Networking
  • Biotechnology and Healthcare Innovations

Join us for two days of knowledge sharing, innovation, and collaboration!
